ARGENT APPLICATION

Keep up to date with your members' attendance and more

What is Argent?

The Argent Application is a software for managing members' record for any club, society etc. It save club executives the hassel of pen and paper and also the overwhelming features of different software that offers alot of unused features. It is very minimal and does the tasks that you only want to be done. It is scalable and can be used in club, society or any other organziation that requires the managing of their members information.

Why Argent

attach_money Track Dues

With Argent you are able to track the dues of each memeber. This way members will be kept up to date with outstanding dues and know how much they have contrubuted so far for that current semester.

people Organize Member Details

Argent has the capability to save and organize members' details very easily and makes fnding a particular member efficient and fast. You are able to search for members easily by different criterias.

With Argent you are able to track the member's attendance record. This will allow you to easily know which memebrs are attending th club or society on a regular basis which will classify them as a full time member.
